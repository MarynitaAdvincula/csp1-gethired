About Us

Our Company

 

The Company was founded by Marynita Fatima Advincula. Utilizing her experiences in recruitment and HR Processes, she was able to consolidate her resources to start this Company. With her respectable reputation, Marynita was able to come up with process and procedures that eventually benefited her Company. Her vision is wrapped around the trust that her clients had with her and without fail she was able to deliver.


Our Company is built from an Idea where in we put first the interest of our partners. It was founded with the values that helps our clients grow as we do too.

 

Our Mission

 

To find, through involvement, commitment and a serious targeted effort, the most qualified candidate for the company’s organization and to give, through dialogue, the most valuable input to the development and growth of the company

We want to be known as:

  • Trustworthy
  • Energetic
  • Flexible
  • Responsible

 

Our Vision

 

We create the best match between the company (which consists of people) and the candidate. We create the basis for a new employee, who with his/her professionalism and personality can contribute and create value in the company (and get success himself)

The right match through counseling and competent sparring, is the value we add – and it occurs between people.
